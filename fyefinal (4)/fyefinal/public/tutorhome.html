First Year Experience

The First Year Experience (FYE) involves a series of activities aimed at developing students holistically to ensure a successful academic life. These activities include orientation for first-year students, peer mentoring, and academic support seminars and workshops.

At orientation, first-year students are welcomed to the university and introduced to the staff, facilities, and services available to them. The program runs for 5 days, combining presentations, talks, and campus tours to help students familiarize themselves with their new environment and prepare them for academic success.

Additionally, seminars and workshops focus on academic literacy, study skills, time-management, financial literacy, and student wellness. These are designed to address areas of need and are facilitated by staff, student mentors, and guest speakers.
